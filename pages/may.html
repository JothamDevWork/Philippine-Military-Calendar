LABOR DAY

Labor Day is celebrated every May 1 in the Philippines. It honors Filipino workers and recognizes their important contributions to the country’s economy and society. It is also known as Araw ng Manggagawa.

Why is it important?

  • It recognizes the hard work and dedication of Filipino workers.
  • It promotes workers’ rights and fair working conditions.
  • It highlights the role of labor in national development.

How do people in the Philippines celebrate?

  • Holding rallies and labor-related activities.
  • Organizing programs that honor workers.
  • Government announcements about wages and benefits.
  • Spending the holiday resting with family.

Labor Day is a celebration of hard work, dignity,
and the strength of Filipino workers.
