INDEPENDENCE DAY

Independence Day is celebrated every June 12 in the Philippines. It marks the country’s declaration of independence from Spanish rule in 1898. It is one of the most important national holidays that highlights Filipino freedom and nationhood.

Why is it important?

  • It celebrates the freedom and sovereignty of the Philippines.
  • It honors the heroes who fought for independence.
  • It strengthens national pride and unity.

How do people in the Philippines celebrate?

  • Attending flag-raising ceremonies and parades.
  • Watching cultural presentations and performances.
  • Displaying the Philippine flag in homes and buildings.
  • Participating in community events and patriotic activities.

Independence Day is a celebration of freedom, bravery, and the enduring spirit
of the Filipino nation.
